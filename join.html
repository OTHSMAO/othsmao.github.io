In the meantime, here are some other ways to reach out!

Join our Discord!

Our Discord is a public and safe for work server where you can have fun and talk about math! We encourage you to join even if you're not in MAO (because you will be soon, whether you like it or not :innocent:)!

Join our Remind!

Remind is our most serious of the three social medias and is the best place to get announcements about club events and competitions!

Follow us on Instagram!

Our Instagram has the most aesthetic and humorous posts that are both math-related and fun! You might even see yourself on there!
