Why should I join MAO?

Here are some reasons you should be part of our fabulous club!

Sharpen your math skills!

Whether you seek help in your academic studies or want to prepare for competitions, MAO offers lessons to help cultivate everyone's skills!

We host various competitions including the AMC, A&M Contest, U of H contest, and even our homemade TMC contest to challenge all skill levels in a variety of subjects!

Stand out for college!

Show your initiative and academic excellence through participating in an honor society recognized by top colleges!

Leadership and awards in MAO can bolster your chances at colleges and camps!

Join a motivated community!

Meet other students who love math as much as you do!

Our Discord is filled with stimulating discussions that will improve your math skills and make you laugh!

Participate in fun events... with food!

Join math games, team challenges, Pi Day parties, and more celebrating your favorite days with fun group activities!

We have free food like cookies, soda, and pizza for members, as well as additional prizes for our activity winners!
